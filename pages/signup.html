What is StreamIntra?

StreamIntra is a video streaming platform for tech enthusiasts and software engineers to share, learn, and keep up with the rapidly changing software industry.

Why StreamIntra?

Websites such as Stack Exchange and medium exist for developers to write content for other developers, but there is a lack of developer first based video streaming platforms.

How will it work?

Users can freely browse the site to watch and upload content they think is helpful, interesting, or funny. Any videos are allowed as long as it pertains to software development. For more information on becoming an early streamer, subscribe with your email.
